CaMEL

Control and Measurement Engineering Laboratory

Mission

CaMEL is dedicated to advancing the frontiers of high-precision measurement and robust control theory. We bridge the gap between complex mathematical modeling and reliable industrial engineering applications.

Research Focus

  • Advanced Control Theory: Robust control, adaptive filtering, and predictive modeling for dynamic systems.
  • Precision Metrology: Development of high-sensitivity sensing architectures and smart signal processing.
  • Embedded Automation: Real-time systems integration for industrial IoT and autonomous hardware.
